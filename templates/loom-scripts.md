# Loom Walkthrough Scripts

Five core walkthrough scripts. Record each as a Loom screen-share video (target: 3–6 minutes each).

---

## Script 1 — Product Overview (3 min)

**Recording setup:** Share your full screen, open a fresh demo account, no notifications.

---

**[INTRO — 0:00–0:20]**
"Hey {{First Name}}, welcome to {{Product Name}}. I'm {{Your Name}} from the Customer Success team. In the next 3 minutes I'll show you exactly where everything lives so you can hit the ground running."

**[DASHBOARD TOUR — 0:20–1:00]**
"This is your main dashboard. The left sidebar has your five core sections: [point to each]. The bell icon top-right is your notifications center — you'll want to check that daily. And this blue button here is how you create anything new."

**[CORE ACTION — 1:00–2:00]**
"Let me show you the most important thing you'll do in {{Product Name}} — creating your first {{core object}}. Click here → [demo the action step by step, narrating each click]. That's it. Takes about 45 seconds once you know where to go."

**[SETTINGS CALLOUT — 2:00–2:30]**
"Before I let you go — head to Settings on Day 1 and make sure your timezone and notification preferences are configured. A lot of people miss this and it causes confusion later."

**[OUTRO — 2:30–3:00]**
"That's the overview. Your next email has a more detailed walkthrough of [feature]. Reply to any email in this sequence if you get stuck — I personally read every reply. Good luck!"

## Script 3 — Team Invite & Permissions (4 min)

**[INTRO — 0:00–0:15]**
"Let's get your team connected. I'll show you how to invite members and set the right permission levels so everyone has access to exactly what they need."

**[INVITE FLOW — 0:15–2:00]**
"Go to Settings → Team Members → Invite. You can invite by email, one at a time or in bulk using a CSV. [Demo both methods]. Invitees get an email immediately with a setup link — it expires in 48 hours, so let your team know to check their inbox."

**[PERMISSIONS OVERVIEW — 2:00–3:15]**
"Permission levels: Admin can see and do everything including billing. Editor can create and modify but not delete or access billing. Viewer is read-only. My recommendation for most teams: one or two Admins, everyone else as Editor unless they're external stakeholders."

**[OUTRO — 3:15–4:00]**
"Once your team is in, I'd suggest setting up your first shared {{workspace/project}} together — that's when {{Product Name}} really starts to click. Your Day 7 email has a guide for that."
