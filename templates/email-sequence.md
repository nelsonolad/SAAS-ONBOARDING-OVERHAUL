## Notes for CSMs

- Send Day 0 automatically via HubSpot workflow trigger on account creation
- Days 1, 3, 7 are automated — review open rates weekly
- Day 10 and Day 14 emails should be **manually reviewed** before sending if the client hasn't completed core milestones
- Flag any client who hasn't opened 3+ emails by Day 5 as **churn risk** → escalate to senior CSM
